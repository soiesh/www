LOTTE ENGINEERING & MACHINERY MFG. has been ambitiously established as the 1st Korean anti-pollution installation constructor in 1973. Since then, Lotte Eng.& Mach. has placed milestones for creating beautiful environment and enriched human lives through and overcoming periodic and technical hardships. Even at the early period of Korean industrialization when our society was at lack of concern in protecting environment. Lotte Eng. & Mach. has acknowledged our social responsibility and practiced our business principle to respect human dignity through harmonious and prosperous lives with nature.
