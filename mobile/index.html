when water supplementation is too often?
ans) regular water supplementation period is between 3 to 6 months, but any type of leakage at pipes inside of boilers can cause water makeup too often.
when heating system does not seem to work at all?
ans) check a select switch of a remote controller. if 'heating-water' mode is selected, turn it to 'general heating'.
how to get rid of frost at refrigerator
ans) first, unplug the power code. second, keep doors open to unfreeze frost. lastly, swipe off ice water and plug the code.
