Our People That Make Us Different

Join us where collaboration, innovation and empowerment help each individual and the company reach new heights

In order to become a "TOP LIVING-SOLUTION PROVIDER," Lotte Technology Corporation is looking for young people to establish the following talents shared and practiced by full-time employees under the mission of "Environment beautifully and Richly Living" and realize their grand dreams with Lotte Technology Corporation. Lotte places a higher value on the possibility of creating the future, not the present, and seeks global talent with passion and responsibility to mature our society with its own growth. We invite the following young people to join Lotte's dream of creating a future where they can enjoy "a world full of love," "a society where freedom breathes," and "a rich life."

  • A young man who is not afraid of failure.
    We invite young people who are not afraid of failure and who have the spirit and determination to challenge success. Creative failure is a privilege of youth. We value the active and active spirit of challenge that can find the possibility of success even in failure more than the stability that we can find the possibility of success even in failure.

  • A young man who knows cooperation and co-prosperity. True talent should have the virtue of cooperating and yielding. I think we can warm up this society by gathering gratitude for the people and society we live together. Developing each person's abilities and skills is also a job for individuals and companies, but Lotte waits for young people who understand their roles as social beings. Invest your young and grand dreams in Lotte. Lotte, which is constantly advancing and growing, will make your beautiful future.
