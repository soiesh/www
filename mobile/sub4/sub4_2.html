Personnel policy

Join us where collaboration, innovation and empowerment help each individual and the company reach new heights

We go through great lengths to hire and develop the best people, and we offer a supportive environment where employees are motivated and empowered to perform at their full potential. Today, we continue pushing the boundaries of what's possible to thrive in a taller, faster, smarter world.
Lotte E&M is An Equal Opportunity, Affirmative Action Employer. All qualified applicants will receive consideration for employment without regard to race, color, religion, sex, sexual orientation, gender identity, national origin, disability or veteran status, age, or any other protected class according to applicable law.

  • HR System
    Based on the familyist organizational culture, Lotte's personnel system seeks support and motivation for all employees to cooperate and enjoy a rich life. It also contributes to the development of the national economy and society by minimizing inflation in manpower management and establishing a manpower management system that enables employees to work stably even in difficult economic conditions.

  • Evaluation
    The evaluation of employees is conducted once or twice a year, and the evaluation results are reflected in movement/position, promotion, compensation, training, etc. Through fair evaluation, performance and rewards are fairly distributed, and goals are managed reasonably, maximizing the competitiveness of the organization. We motivate our employees with a corresponding reward for excellent personnel. This greatly helps the company achieve its vision and goals, and contributes to securing talented people and providing opportunities for employees to grow.
